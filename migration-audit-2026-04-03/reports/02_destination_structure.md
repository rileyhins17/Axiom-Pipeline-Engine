## Notes on refinement

- The requested top-level structure is valid for this machine and was created without overcomplication.
- Recommended operational refinement:
  - Use `Review` subfolders inside the likely destination when confidence is medium.
  - Keep heavy non-sync material in `Archive` unless there is a specific reason to stage it for Drive.
  - Keep secrets and credential exports out of Google Drive.

## Recommended usage

- `Axiom`
  - active business repos, lead exports, operations docs, website work, brand assets
- `School`
  - current term assignments, older course packages, study PDFs, resume/co-op documents
- `Personal`
  - media, creative projects, personal device/reference items
- `Archive`
  - legacy dumps, duplicate review, large-file review, low-confidence sorting, software payloads not meant for cloud sync
